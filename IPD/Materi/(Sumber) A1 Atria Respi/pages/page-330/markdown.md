# Take Home Points

## Pneumokoniosis

- Etiologi: paparan inhalasi debu/mineral kronis, berkaitan dengan pekerjaan
- Diagnosis: Riwayat paparan + foto thoraks
- Asbestosis: pleural plaque, fibrosis linear
- Silicosis: eggshell calcification
- CWP: infiltrat milier/nodular lapang paru atas
- Berliliosis: pembesaran KGB hilar
- Byssinosis: gejala Monday disease
- Aluminosis: honeycombing, infiltrat nodul/difus
- Tata laksana: hindari paparan (ganti pekerjaan)
