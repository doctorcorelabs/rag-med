# Juvenile Idiopathic Arthritis

Manifestasi Klinis:

|  Tipe | Pola Sendi | Manifestasi Ekstra-artikular  |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  Oligoartikular JIA (50%) | Melibatkan ≤ 5 sendi; Asimetris, sendi weight bearing (lutut, pergelangan kaki) | Uveitis anterior kronis bilateral  |
|  Poliartikular JIA seronegatif (30%) | Melibatkan > 5 sendi; Simetris/asimetris, vertebra servikal dan sendi TMJ | Uveitis anterior kronis bilateral  |
|  Poliartikular JIA seropositif (<10%) | Melibatkan > 5 sendi; Simetris | Nodul rheumatoid  |
