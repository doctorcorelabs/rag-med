# Bell's Criteria: Staging NEC

|  Staging | Gambaran Klinis | Gambaran Radiologi | Diagnosis  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
|  Stage I | Distensi usus, mual-muntah, feses dengan darah (+) | Dilatasi usus | Suspek NEC  |
|  Stage II | Stage I + bising usus menghilang, dapat disertai nyeri tekan | Ditemukan pneumatosis intestinalis | Proven NEC  |
|  Stage III | Hipotensi, apnea, tanda peritonitis | Dapat ditemukan pneumoperitoneum | Advanced NEC  |
