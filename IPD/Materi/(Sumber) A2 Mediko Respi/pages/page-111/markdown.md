- Tekanan hidrostatik → CHF
- Permeabilitas vascular → Pneumonia
- Tekanan negative intrapleura → Atelektasis
- Tekanan onkotik → Sindroma Nefrotik
- Drainase pembuluh limfe → Karsinoma

## Causes of TRANSUDATIVE Pleural Effusion → MARCH

- Meig’s syndrome
- Albumine (low)
- Renal failure
- Cirrhosis/ CCF
- Heart failure/ Hypothyroidism

## Causes of EXUDATIVE Pleural Effusion → MYOPIA

- Malignancy (lung cancer, lymphoma)
- Yellow nail syndrome
- Oesophageal rupture
- Pancreatitis/ PE
- Infection (pneumonia, TB)
- Autoimmune (SLE, RA, sarcoidosis)
