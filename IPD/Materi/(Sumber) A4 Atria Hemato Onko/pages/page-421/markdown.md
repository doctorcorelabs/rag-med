## Skoring:

- Skor SOFA (sequential organ failure assessment):
- Tujuan: menilai **risiko mortalitas** pada pasien di ICU
- Skoring:

|  Skor SOFA | Mortalitas  |
| --- | --- |
|  0 – 6 | <10%  |
|  7 – 9 | 15 – 20%  |
|  10 – 12 | 40 – 50%  |
|  13 – 14 | 50 – 60%  |
|  15 | >80%  |
|  15 – 24 | >90%  |

- Sepsis: bukti/suspek infeksi + ≥2 poin pada skor SOFA
