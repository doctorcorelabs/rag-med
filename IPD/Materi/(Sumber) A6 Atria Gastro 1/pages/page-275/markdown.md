## Abses Hepar Amoeba

- Etiologi → Entamoeba histolytica
- Manifestasi klinis → Demam, Ludwig's sign, hepatomegali
- Pemeriksaan penunjang
- Invasif → aspirasi hepar → anchovy paste
- Non invasif → USG hepar → lesi soliter lobus kanan
- Tata laksana → Metronidazole 3x500-750 mg selama 5-10 hari
